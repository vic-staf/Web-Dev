Your Itinerary Highlights

Day 1: Arrival in Helsinki

Welcome to Helsinki, a coastal city blending modern design with historic landmarks.

  • Visit Helsinki Cathedral, Temppeliaukio Church, and Market Square.
  • Explore the design district with boutique stores and Finnish craftsmanship.

Day 2: Journey to Lapland

Fly to Rovaniemi, the capital of Finnish Lapland and Santa's official hometown.

  • Visit Santa Claus Village and cross the Arctic Circle.
  • Enjoy a Finnish sauna and local delicacies like reindeer stew.

Day 3: Arctic Adventures

Experience thrilling activities in the snowy wilderness of Lapland.

  • Go husky sledding or take a reindeer sleigh ride.
  • Chase the Northern Lights on a guided aurora safari.

Day 4: Finnish Lakeland

Relax in the serene Lakeland region, surrounded by pristine lakes and forests.

  • Stay in a lakeside cabin with a private sauna.
  • Enjoy canoeing, fishing, or simply soaking in nature's tranquility.

Day 5: Culinary Delights and Farewell

Savor Finnish cuisine and shop for unique souvenirs before departing.

  • Try salmon soup, Karelian pies, and cloudberry desserts.
  • Shop for Moomin mugs, Arctic jewelry, and Finnish wool products.

Why Choose Finland?

  • Breathtaking landscapes: From Lapland to the serene Lakeland.
  • Seasonal wonders: Midnight Sun in summer, Northern Lights in winter.
  • Cultural richness: Sauna culture, Finnish traditions, and innovative design.
  • Sustainability: Eco-friendly tourism practices.
